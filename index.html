Featured Online Course

科学技术哲学专题 (Topics in Philosophy of Science and Technology)
This course bridges science, philosophy, and religion across Chinese and Western traditions. It covers the history and philosophy of science, relativity, quantum mechanics, the Big Bang theory, and their connections with Chinese traditional thought, Buddhism, and Daoism.
